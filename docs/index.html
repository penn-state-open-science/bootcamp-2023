About Bootcamp 2023

These pages document and describe the 2023 Open Science Bootcamp held at the University Park campus of the Pennsylvania State University August 9-11, 2023 at the Marriott Foundation Building.

Logistics

Location

Most in-person events at Bootcamp 2023 will be held in the newly renovated Marriott Foundation Building on the Penn State University Park campus. The Marriott Foundation Building is located just north of the Nittany Lion Inn, near the intersection of Park Avenue and Atherton Street.

Figure 1: Marriott Foundation Building

Figure 2: Marriott Foundation Building location from https://www.map.psu.edu

Remote participants

Bootcamp 2023 will be streamed online at the following URL:

https://psu.zoom.us/bootcamp-2023/

All sessions will be recorded and made publicly available at a later date.

Acknowledgements

We gratefully acknowledge the Department of Psychology, the Child Study Center (CSC), Laureen Teti, and Nancy English for support.
